Johannesburg Chamber of Commerce

Fostering Business Growth in the Heart of South Africa

Site Purpose

The Johannesburg Chamber of Commerce serves as a central hub for local businesses in Johannesburg, South Africa. Our primary goals are to promote business growth, provide networking opportunities, and offer valuable resources and information on the city's economic development.

Scenarios

  • What upcoming events support local businesses and offer networking opportunities?
  • Where can I find contact details for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the recent population growth in the Johannesburg area?
